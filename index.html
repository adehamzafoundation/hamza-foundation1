Our Mission

At Adé & Morenike Hamza Foundation, we are dedicated to uplifting underprivileged communities in Africa, focusing on empowering children and widows through food, education, and support.

Our Programs

Food Security Initiative

We provide food to communities facing hunger, ensuring that no one has to go to bed hungry.

Education for All

We offer scholarships, school supplies, and mentorship to help children access education.
